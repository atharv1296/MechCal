About the Engineering Suite

The Atlas Copco Mechanical Calculator Suite is an internal engineering platform built to standardise and accelerate precision calculations across departments.

Engineers can create purpose-built calculators for any mechanical computation — from shaft diameter sizing and torque analysis to load-factor assessments — without writing a single line of code.

Each calculator supports dynamic input fields, named constants, dropdown selection menus and formula-driven output expressions. Results are automatically logged to the history for traceability and engineering sign-off.
